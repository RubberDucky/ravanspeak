# RavanSpeak

**On-device subtitle studio for macOS.** Transcribe video in Farsi (and other
languages) with Whisper on the Apple Neural Engine, then translate, transliterate,
overlay two languages at once, edit, and export — plus an interactive word-by-word
**Reader** for studying. Everything runs on your Mac.

## Install

1. **[Download the latest release.](https://github.com/RubberDucky/ravanspeak/releases/latest)**
2. Unzip and drag **RavanSpeak** into your **Applications** folder.
3. Open it from Launchpad or Spotlight.

Once installed, RavanSpeak **updates itself** — it checks for new versions and
installs them in place, so you only download manually this one time.

## What it does

- **Transcribe on-device** (Apple Neural Engine) — you pick the spoken language.
- **Translate & transliterate** subtitles (free on-device model, or your OpenAI key).
- **Dual-language overlay** — two subtitle tracks stacked on the video.
- **Edit every track** inline, with per-line timing; export to `.srt` / `.vtt`.
- **Reader** — paste English or Farsi text for an interactive, word-by-word study
  guide: tappable Farsi / transliteration / English, hide-and-reveal practice,
  spoken pronunciation, saved vocab, and HTML export.

## Notes

- `.mp4`, `.mov`, `.m4v` and common audio are supported (convert `.mkv` first).
- Your library, subtitles, study passages, and settings stay on your Mac and are
  never touched by updates.
